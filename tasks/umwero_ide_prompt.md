## Project Overview

Project Name: **UMWERO**

Meaning: In Kinyarwanda, *Umwero* refers to **fertility, productivity,
and richness of the soil**.\
The project represents a smart agriculture platform designed to help
farmers in **Rwanda and across Africa** improve crop health, detect
diseases early, and understand the nutrients their plants need.

The website is **not the AI system itself**. It is a **modern prototype
website that explains the UMWERO project, its vision, features, and
impact on African agriculture**.

The concept is inspired by the plant disease detection platform Plantix,
but UMWERO focuses specifically on **African crops, African soil
conditions, and farmers who often have limited internet access**.
